Bio

Amir Shmuel received his Ph.D. degree in 1999 from the Weizmann Institute of Science. He worked in Center for MR Research at university of Minnesota, Max Planck Institute for Biological Cybernetics, European Molecular Biology Organization as post-doctoral.

In 2007, Dr. Shmuel joined the Department of Biomedical Engineering and Dept. of Physiology of Mc.Gill University. Since then he has been Canada Research Chair in Neuroscience and Brain Imaging

In 2013, Dr. Shmuel was promoted to the rank of associate professor at Biomedical Engineering
